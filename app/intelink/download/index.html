Data downloading

This section gives the procedure to download the connected data from DEBUT devices using INTELINK. Before you start, make sure that you have turned on the Bluetooth on your mobile.

  1. Tap icon at the bottom of the App.
    App lists all of your devices. You can see the device number, MAC address and UUID of each device.
    icon represents a gateway device, i.e. HUB.
    icon represents a terminal device, i.e. FLEX, LEGO, MINI or NANO.
  2. Select the device you need to download data.
    You can identify the device by the MAC address printed on the back (for some devices, the last four digits of MAC address is printed). You can select multiple devices by long pressing the device.
  3. Tap Next.
    If you select a single device, you don’t see this step.
  4. In the pop-up menu, tap Data downloading.
    App starts establishing connection with the device using INTELINK. If the device is too far away and cannot be reached by the phone, DEBUT App will automatically start to connect to the relay devices (HUB or QUEST) nearby after 60 seconds, and then connect to the device through the relay. You can tap Skip and the App immediately starts connecting to the relay device.

    After the device is connected, App downloads the data connected by this device automatically.
    Note：Before using INTELINK to connect to other devices through DEBUT HUB, you must add the device to the whitelist of the HUB. For more information, see Adding terminal.

DEBUT App can connect to 4 devices at a time. If you select 5 or more devices, App starts to connect to the remaining devices automatically after finishing the operations on the first 4 devices.
