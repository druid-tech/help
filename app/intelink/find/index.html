Device finding

This section gives the procedure to find your device using INTELINK. Before you start, make sure that you have turned on the Bluetooth on your mobile and your mobile is not muted.

  1. Tap icon at the bottom of the App.
    App lists all of your devices. You can see the device number, MAC address and UUID of each device.
    icon represents a gateway device, i.e. HUB.
    icon represents a terminal device, i.e. FLEX, LEGO, MINI or NANO.
  2. Select the device you need to download data.
    You can identify the device by the MAC address printed on the back (for some devices, the last four digits of MAC address is printed). You can select only one device at a time.
  3. In the pop-up menu, tap Device finding.
    App starts searching for nearby devices and your mobile starts ringing. When you are getting closer to the device, the ring is getting louder.
