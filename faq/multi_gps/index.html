How to check GPS tracks of multiple devices on the same map？

  1. On the main menu, click Device > Terminal.

  2. In the terminal list, click the SN of the device you want to view.

  3. On the device information page, click Track.
    DEBUT Data Center opens a new window to display the GPS track of the device.

  4. In the search bar of the track page, enter the SN of the device you want to view.


    The search result is displayed in a drop-down list.

  5. In the drop-down list, click the device.
    The GPS track of that device is displayed on the same map in a different color. DEBUT Data Center can display GPS tracks of 5 devices at the most on the same map.
